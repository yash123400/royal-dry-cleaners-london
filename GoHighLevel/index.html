Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence in Clerkenwell

Royal Dry Cleaners has been a pillar of the Clerkenwell community since 1982. As a family-run business, we believe that true dry cleaning is an art form. Unlike many modern services, we process every single garment in-store, ensuring total control over the quality and care your items receive.

Whether it's a cherished wedding dress, a tailored suit, or your everyday business wear, our team—led by Master Tailor Ali—applies decades of expertise to every stitch and stain.
